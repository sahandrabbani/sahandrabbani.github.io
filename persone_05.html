Liza Paints Pictures

Far away from my world,
I trudged through an eternal wave
To a strange place--
Distant from my circle of friends,
Full of unanswered questions--
With nothing but a crashed motor.

Mars or Aldebaran:
Lonely stars, truly,
Lacking the company of friends.

Liza always longed to paint pictures,
And so did I, but not with such passion,
So my box of paints
I abandoned, along with my desire,
But Liza paints pictures still.

I take a stroll one evening,
A ticket and some change in my pocket,
Enough to sustain me.
There is in this land
Only one with the right quality
To understand me in full.
Criticism or reproach?
Idea or proposal?
Contact me by phone.

Liza always longed to paint pictures,
And so did I, but not with such passion,
So my box of paints
I abandoned, along with my desire,
But Liza paints pictures still.

Perhaps I knew right from the start.
Perhaps, despite all, I was correct.

Liza always longed to paint pictures,
And so did I, but not with such passion,
So my box of paints
I abandoned, along with my desire,
But Liza paints pictures still.

Chances are that Liza paints pictures still.
