Two Men

The dream recurs,
Never fades;
The image sharpens
The more I fight it.
Hey, my mind is beset by
Impressions, memories; the dream persists.

Two men standing on the earth
Below the blue sky:
A three-day affair,
Then life goes on.

To our secret garden
You resort by night,
But by morning
The intimacy is lost.
Hey, I desire nothing,
Save that you confess: we longed for each other
